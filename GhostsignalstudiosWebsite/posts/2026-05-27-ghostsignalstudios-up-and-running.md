Ghost Signal Studios has officially launched! We are still getting our bearings and focused on our future projects. This first post will serve as some background as well as minor announcements for future projects. As it stands right now, Ghost Signal Studios is a one-man band, but I hope to pull in some more talent very soon. I started this company a little earlier in my college career, where I am currently studying cyber security. At first it was just a way to hopefully round out my portfolio. Later, though, I discovered that I was developing skills to build my own projects along the way.

Right now the plan is to develop three main projects. The main one, which will hopefully be finished around the end of this year, will be an adventure point-and-click horror game. The second project still coming together will be an arms dealing video game centered around making deals and avoiding rivals. Finally, the last project will be the TTRPG known currently as "Century." Outside of these projects, there will be a weekly podcast every week on YouTube, which is linked on our homepage. The first episode will be this Monday, June 1st. The topic of discussion Monday will be more information on the initial horror game. I hope to see everyone there.

\-Eric
