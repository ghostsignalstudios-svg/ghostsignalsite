The first release from this studio has officially started production. The game will be a point-and-click horror game focused mainly around moving through a house and discovering its secrets. The upcoming podcast this week will focus mostly on what players can expect from the game. As of now, the release is slated for sometime at the end of this year, but as we get closer that will become a little more set in stone.

Overall, I am really excited to start production. The podcast and the dev videos will be held on the YouTube channel, which you can find from the homepage on the site.

-Eric
